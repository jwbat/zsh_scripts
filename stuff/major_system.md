### Major System

| Digit | Letters       | Memory Aid                        |
|-------|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| 0     | S, Z          | Zero starts with Z                |
| 1     | T, D          | T has 1 downstroke                |
| 2     | N             | N has 2 downstrokes               |
| 3     | M             | M has 3 downstrokes               |
| 4     | R             | Four ends in R                    |
| 5     | L             | L is 50 in Roman numerals         |
| 6     | J, SH, CH     | J is a mirrored 6                 |
| 7     | K, G (hard)   | K looks like two 7s back to back  |
| 8     | F, V          | Cursive f resembles an 8          |
| 9     | P, B          | P is a mirrored 9                 |
